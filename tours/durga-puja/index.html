Kolkata X Durga Puja Photo Tour

"Through your lens, witness the unwavering devotion that echoes in every prayer offered during Durga Puja."

Embark on a mesmerising journey through the enchanting streets of Kolkata, a city renowned for its deep soul and captivating charm. This photography tour offers a unique opportunity to capture the essence of Kolkata and immerse yourself in the vibrant festivities of the renowned Durga Puja. Known as the City of Joy, Kolkata boasts a rich cultural heritage, a perfect blend of old-world traditions and modern vibrancy.

During the enthusiastic preparation time for the Durga Puja festival, Kolkata comes alive with an air of anticipation and excitement. Ordinary families and diverse communities unite to create an atmosphere teeming with artistry, vibrancy, and devotion. As a photographer, you will witness firsthand the transformation of the city into a visual wonderland.

The streets of Kolkata become a kaleidoscope of colours, as beautifully crafted idols of Goddess Durga find their homes in elaborately decorated pandals. These architectural marvels, adorned with intricate details and innovative designs, serve as a backdrop for stunning photographic compositions.

As you wander through the bustling lanes and witness the grandeur of the Puja preparations, you will capture the spirit of celebration that permeates every nook and cranny of the city. From the passionate artisans perfecting their creations to the joyous faces of devotees engrossed in prayer, each moment presents an opportunity to encapsulate the magic of Kolkata during this auspicious festival.



Captivating Pandals: A Photographer's Delight during the Durga Puja Festival


Every year, Kolkata's talented artisans and creative minds come together to construct pandals that transcend mere temporary shelters. They transform into architectural marvels, each competing to be more awe-inspiring than the last. These pandals become a canvas for artistic expression, where imagination knows no bounds.

The pandals of Kolkata during Durga Puja showcase a fusion of traditional and contemporary designs, seamlessly blending the city's rich heritage with modern influences. From intricate terracotta work to ornate sculptures, the attention to detail is awe-inspiring. As a photographer, you have the opportunity to capture the nuances of craftsmanship that adorn these structures. For photographers seeking to capture the essence of Kolkata's Durga Puja festival, the captivating pandals are an absolute must-see. These temporary structures, meticulously crafted and adorned with breathtaking artistry, serve as the centrepiece of the festival, offering a visual feast for the lens.

Itinerary for Kolkata X Durga Puja Photography Tour

Arrival - Day 0

  • Arrival in India: Everyone can reach during the day time and rest at the nice hotel in central Kolkata near the Park Street area of Kolkata till dinner.
  • Dinner: Meet fellow photographers and receive an orientation briefing by Shivam.

Day 1

  • Begin early to witness the arrival of fresh flowers and capture the vibrant atmosphere of one of India's largest flower markets. The market's frenzy offers a challenging yet rewarding photography opportunity. The nearby Hooghly River adds picturesque moments and insights into the rituals and traditions.
  • Explore the older part of the city, known as old Calcutta, which showcases a rich blend of tradition and culture. Wander through the labyrinthine lanes, documenting the captivating juxtaposition of the old and the new.

Day 2

  • Morning visit to the outdoor wrestling hall near the Howrah Bridge where Howrah Bridge itself becomes the best backdrop.
  • In the evening, visit Kalighat and the renowned Kali temple to experience the spiritual ambiance and witness the rituals.

Day 3

  • Streets of Burrabazar Area, Metal market, vibes of fruit market will give you a perfect opportunity to capture the essence of amazing labours in these areas.
  • Kumortuli, the bustling neighbourhood where artisans are working diligently to complete the clay idols for the festival.

Day 4

  • Early Morning Rituals: Witness the start of the day's rituals at the river, capturing the spiritual activities and devotion and Observing the puja (worship) ceremony Raj Bari, gaining an intimate insight into their traditions and customs.
  • Explore the modern southern part of the city, which boasts stunning Art Deco architecture and unique renditions of the neighbourhood. Experience the vibrant adda culture against the backdrop of the festival and architectural marvels. Visit some of Kolkata's famous Pandals located in the southern neighbourhoods.

Day 5

  • We start late in the evening to embark on a journey to various Pandals (temporary shrines), each offering exquisite and sometimes quirky creations. Immerse yourself in the artistic brilliance and vibrant energy of the festival. On this day we will do our photo tour till late night hence we are keeping only the evening session for this day and next day.

Day 6

  • Witness the peak of religious and ritual fervour as Ashtami marks the beginning of the end of the Durga Puja. Attend a traditional Durga Arati, where you will be mesmerised with the colours of the butter lamps.

Day 7

  • Check-Out: Guests have their rooms until the checkout time. If you wish to extend your stay, please inform us in advance.

Disclaimer : The itinerary is subject to change based on local conditions and availability. The focus of this photography tour is to capture the intensity of Puja preparations, the vibrant Pandals, and various cultural experiences associated with the Durga Puja festival.
